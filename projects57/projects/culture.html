Akwa Ibom Culture

Traditional Attire

Men often wear wrappers with long shirts and hats, while women dress in colorful George wrappers, blouses, and head ties during cultural ceremonies. These attires reflect the elegance and pride of the people.

Festivals

The state celebrates vibrant festivals like the Ekpe Festival, filled with traditional dances, masquerades, and music, attracting visitors from across Nigeria and beyond.

Marriage Rites

Traditional marriage is a grand celebration where the groom presents items to the bride’s family in a rich cultural ceremony filled with dance, music, and traditional blessings.

Food

Popular delicacies include Afang Soup, Edikang Ikong, and Fisherman Soup — rich in flavor and often shared during festivals and family gatherings.
