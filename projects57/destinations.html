Top Tourist Destinations in Akwa Ibom

Explore some of the most beautiful and historically rich locations across Akwa Ibom State.

Ibeno Beach

One of the longest beaches in West Africa, Ibeno Beach offers stunning ocean views, white sand, and a relaxing atmosphere perfect for visitors.

Ibom Plaza

Located in the heart of Uyo, Ibom Plaza is both a commercial center and a cultural landmark, known for its beautiful sculptures and vibrant atmosphere.

Lord Lugard’s Residence

This colonial-era building is a piece of history, once occupied by Lord Lugard during the British rule. A must-see for history lovers.

National Museum

The National Museum in Uyo preserves artifacts and historical items from Akwa Ibom and beyond. Learn about local traditions and Nigeria's heritage.
